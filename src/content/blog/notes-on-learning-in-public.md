---
title: "Notes on Learning in Public"
date: 2026-07-05
description: "Writing about what I'm learning, before I feel ready to."
tags: ["writing", "meta"]
---

There's a trap in waiting until you're an expert to write about something:
you forget what it was like to not understand it.

So I'm trying the opposite — writing things down while they're still fresh
and slightly confusing. The posts won't be authoritative. They'll be honest.

If a future reader (probably me) finds an error, that's not embarrassing.
That's the system working.
